Reviewer matching survey    sodestream

Thank you for participating! This survey is part of the SodeStream project. We are building a reviewer recommendation engine for the IETF. This is intended to hep Working Group chairs by automatically suggesting available reviewers for given drafts based on a composition of interests and expertise. Here is how we need your help!

Step 1. Clicking on a draft name below opens an annotation form for it in a new tab. It will contain the top 50 candidates provided by the current experimental review recommendation system. You can rate each candidate as:

  • not suitable
  • below average
  • average
  • above average
  • very suitable

Step 2. Add metadata: optionally, enter more useful information (e.g., comments, information about your role/position in the IETF, or good candidates missing from the list)

Step 3. Click "Save" to submit your annotations and close the tab.

Notes:

  • You can click on a name of a candidate to open their Datatracker page in a new tab.
  • The list is not sorted by the score of the current reccomendation system, rather it contains top 50 candidates in random order. Do not let the position of a candidate bias your assessment.
  • Try to make your decision for a candidate independent of the rest (e.g., after a long streak of scoring candidates as "2", someone you might otherwise give a "4" to might seem like a "5" instead. Please try to avoid this bias.)
  • All fields are optional (including entering your email below). However, even anonymously, the more information you supply the better.
  • Rank reviewers by expertise. If you feel someone is unsuitable for other reasons (conflict of interest, illness, etc.), base your assesment on their expertise only (but you can add a comment for each reviewer).
  • Drafts you successfully submitted will be marked as DONE! (remembered in a cookie expiring in 30 days, if this functionality breaks don't worry, your annotations are still on the server).
For any questions or comments please contact: m.karan@qmul.ac.uk
