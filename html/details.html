IETF Draft-Reviewer Matching Survey    sodestream

This survey is part of the SodeStream project. The motivation for producing this this labeled data set is that it would enable the following.

  • To explore what makes a good reviewer.
  • To develop a tool that can give advice to working group chairs, IESG members, and those selecting directorate reviewers. Especially in cases requiring cross-area review where a draft could benefit from a reviewer with complementarty expertise.
  • To infer relevant reviewers based on their public contributions to IETF. rather than the personal knowledge of the chairs. This may help to surface good reviewer candidates who are not necessarily well-known in the community.

The candidates you are rating are obtained by using Natural Language Processing methodology to compare email text of a person (to public mailing lists) with the text of the draft.
